Note on 2009/10:
Summing the non-fire false alarm incident categories for all FRSs may not sum to England totals due to data inconsistencies.
Note on Hampshire FRA
Before 2017/18, Hampshire FRS did not record medical co-responding incidents in the IRS. They are currently undertaking a project to upload this data for 2017/18 to 2020/21.
This was not completed in time for publication on 27 August 2024 but will be included in the quarterly release once available.
General note:
Fire data are collected by the Incident Recording System (IRS) which collects information on all incidents attended by fire and rescue services. For a variety of reasons some records take longer
than others for fire services to upload to the IRS and therefore totals are constantly being amended (by relatively small numbers).
