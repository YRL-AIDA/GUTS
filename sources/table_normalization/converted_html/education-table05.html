All attributes/fields (Age Band, Region, LA, LAD, & PCON) are included at all times in the PivotTable tool or the filters (i.e. they are all ticked in the PivotTable fields pane).
All the attributes/fields must have 'Total' selected (unless specifically filtering on a particular subset of an attribute/field).
None of the attributes must have '(All)' selected, unless in the main body of the table itself.
